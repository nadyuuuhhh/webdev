MY FAVORITE POP ARTIST

My favorite pop artists are Sabrina Carpenter, Taylor Swift, and Ariana Grande. Sabrina Carpenter’s music always feels so relatable and fun. Taylor Swift’s storytelling abilities draw me in, and I love how she evolves with each album. Ariana Grande has an incredible vocal range and brings so much energy to her performances. Each of these artists inspires me in different ways through their music and creativity.
